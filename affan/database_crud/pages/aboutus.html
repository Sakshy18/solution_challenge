Who are we & What do we aim:

"sharing education, sharing a book...that is what will change the world "

We are a bunch of university students who believe everyone has a right to education and are willing to do our best for the same.
Hence we have come up with an idea of reselling books platform that allows the user to resell them, he/she can chat with the buyers and sell them accordingly. User will be able to buy second-hand books at a reasonable price and hence everyone will be able to access knowledge in a budget friendly way. Hence implementing 'Education to all'.
